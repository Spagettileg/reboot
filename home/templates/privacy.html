Message from ReBoot

Re-Boot is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data processing practices and your options regarding the ways in which your personal data is used. If you have any requests concerning your personal information or any queries with regard to our processing please contact us.

Information collected

Re-Boot collects the details provided by you on registration together with information Re-Boot learn about you from your use of our service and your visits to our web site and potentially other sites accessible from them. Re-Boot also collect information about the transactions you undertake including details of payment methods used.

Re-Boot may collect additional information in connection with your participation in any promotions or competitions offered by us and information you provide when giving us feedback or completing profile forms. Re-Boot also monitor customer traffic patterns and site use which enables Re-Boot to improve the service Re-Boot provides.

Use of your information and your preferences

Re-Boot will use your information to provide and personalise our service and to detect and prevent fraud. Re-Boot will also use your contact details to communicate with you. Re-Boot may use your information to send you offers and news about Re-Boot and services or those of other carefully selected companies which Re-Boot think may be of interest to you. Re-Boot may contact you by post, email or telephone for these purposes.

Please note that there may be instances where it may be necessary for Re-Boot to communicate with you, in any event, for administrative or operational reasons relating to Re-Boots' service.

Disclosures of your information

Your information may be passed to and used by Re-Boot. Re-Boot will never pass your personal information to anyone else, except where Re-Boot are required or permitted to do so by law, for any successors in title to our business and suppliers that process information on our behalf both in the UK and abroad. We may also use and disclose information in aggregate (so that no individual customers are identified) for marketing and strategic development purposes.
